Who We Are

ANCOP is the network of international organisations that supports the work of CFC ANCOP Global Foundation, Inc. in the Philippines and other Third World countries. ANCOP is presently in more than 50 locations around the world.

ANCOP, an acronym for "ANswering the Cry Of the Poor, is a non-profit founded by Couples for Christ (CFC). CFC is a global lay organisation recognized by Vatican for its work of the renewal and strengthening of Christian family life and values. It is present in over 100 countries around the world.

ANCOP lnternational Bermuda is a duly registered charitable organisation under the Charities Act, 2014 with charity registration number #802.
