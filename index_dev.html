Our Story

Answering the cry of the poor

ANCOP, an acronym for "ANswering the Cry Of the Poor, is a non-profit founded by Couples for Christ (CFC). CFC is a global lay organisation recognized by Vatican for its work of the renewal and strengthening of Christian family life and values. It is present in over 100 countries around the world.


ANCOP Bermuda is a duly registered charitable organization under the Charities Act 2014, with charity registration No. 802.

Our Mission

To make better quality of life of the poor families and to improve the communities where they live through a sustainable and holistic approach.

ANCOP Walk

Faith in action

The ANCOP Walk is a global annual event of ANCOP that raises funds for its various programs including SHELTER, CHILD SPONSORSHIP, and HUMANITARIAN RELIEF.

Individuals and groups participate in the walk, generously giving donations and helping solicit funds for ANCOP.

Our Causes

Child sponsorship

The ANCOP Child Sponsorship Program is a one-to-one sponsor-to-scholar connection that offers a comprehensive program benefit package that includes education, healthcare, and socio-cultural-spiritual Development for sponsored children and their parents.
